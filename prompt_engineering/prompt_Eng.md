Prompt engineering is the practice of designing, refining, and optimizing inputs (prompts) to guide Large Language Models (LLMs) toward producing accurate, relevant, and high-quality outputs. It involves structuring natural language, providing context, and setting constraints to improve AI performance on tasks like text generation, coding, and reasoning.
